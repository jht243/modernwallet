  - `sections: Array<{heading: string; content: string}>` — body sections
  - `faqs: FAQ[]` — ≥4 FAQs
  - `sources?: Source[]` — primary sources
  - `relatedSlugs?: string[]` — sibling comparison slugs
  - `calculatorLinks?: Array<{label: string; href: string}>` — CTA to calculators
